What is Listing Quota

What is Listing Quota on MokeSell

Listing quota refers to the number of listings you can create or mark as active within a specific time frame. The duration of this time frame can vary depending on the country of your marketplace.

How does Listing Quota work?

Each seller is allocated a certain number of free listings in each category. This free quota refreshes periodically, the frequency may differ depending on the country of your marketplace.

A quota is consumed once a listing is successfully listed as active. Deleting or marking a listing as sold/reserved does not free up the used quota.

When a seller uses up their free quota in a category, they can either purchase a MokeBiz subscription, additional custom quota, or wait for the free quota to refresh to list more items.

Note: Due to market dynamics, listing quotas vary by category and are different in each country where MokeSell operates. We have 2 different quota systems that apply to (a) the General goods categories, and (b) Autos, Property, Jobs, and Services categories.

Why do we have Listing Quota?

  • Encourage sellers to maintain their inventory with fresh, up to date listings to increase the quality of listings in the marketplace.
  • Provides visibility for sellers' listings which helps their items sell faster.
  • Reduce the number of duplicate listings, spam or low quality listings to make it easy for buyers to find their desired items in the marketplace.
