How do I contact the Support Team?

Solving problems is a Core Value here at Mokesell

We want all our Mokesellers to get the help they need quickly and efficiently. If you haven't already checked out the articles available on our Help Centre, we encourage you to do a quick article search to see if the answers you're looking for can be found!

If you need further assistance, message us! Messaging Bot is available from Monday to Sunday for users in Hong Kong, Malaysia, Philippines, Singapore, and Taiwan. Select the floating message button** at the bottom right-hand corner of this page to start messaging us. Alternatively, we invite you to submit a request to us – we'll be here to help!

** Please check if there is any ad-blocking extension enabled in your browser, as it may block the message button from showing up.

To ensure that all our Mokesellers experience the best support possible, here are some useful tips to help us solve your problems quicker:

  • If you're facing technical problems on the app, make sure that you're on the latest version of the app and try out these troubleshooting steps.
  • If you're having problems dealing with a buyer or seller, always provide a background of the issue along with the other party's Mokesell username and listing title when reporting a user.
  • If you see a listing that is not in line with our Listing Rules, here is how you can report a listing.
  • If you simply have an idea or a suggestion to improve our app, we want to hear anything and everything from our community that will help us solve problems and continuously improve the Mokesell product.
